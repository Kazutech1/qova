# Qova Design System & Principles

This document serves as the source of truth for the visual identity and design principles of **Qova**. It defines the "Circle Elegant" aesthetic, which balances traditional community finance with modern, premium technology.

---

## 1. Design Philosophy: "Circle Elegant"

Qova's design is built on the principle of **Traditional Sophistication**. It aims to feel as reliable as a multi-generational savings circle while being as efficient as a global financial protocol.

### Core Principles:
- **Trust through Clarity:** High whitespace and clean layouts to ensure users feel in control of their funds.
- **Premium Reliability:** A sophisticated color palette and high-end typography that move away from "cheap" fintech aesthetics.
- **Human-Centric Security:** Using friendly yet professional language and clear visual cues (locks, badges) to reassure users.
- **The "Circle" Motif:** Representing the communal, cyclical nature of Ajo through geometric visualizations.

## 3. Typography

Qova uses a dual-font system to contrast traditional elegance with modern precision.

### Headings (Serif)
- **Font:** `Playfair Display`
- **Weight:** Bold / SemiBold
- **Usage:** Screen titles (H1, H2) and hero sections.
- *Why:* Evokes authority, tradition, and timeless trust.

### Body & UI (Sans-serif)
- **Font:** `Inter`
- **Weight:** Regular / Medium / SemiBold
- **Usage:** Inputs, body text, buttons, and data visualizations.
- *Why:* Modern, highly legible, and precise.

## 4. UI Structural Elements

### Border Radius
- **Global Radius:** `8px` to `16px`.
- **Buttons:** `12px` (Large) or `8px` (Medium).
- **Inputs:** `12px`.
- *Why:* A "balanced" radius that feels friendlier than sharp corners but more structured than extreme rounding.

### Shadows & Depth
- **Soft Lift:** `shadowOpacity: 0.05`, `shadowRadius: 4`.
- Used sparingly on white cards (`Surface`) to provide depth without clutter.

### Border Strokes
- **Standard:** `1px` (E2E8F0).
- **Active/Focused:** `1.5px` to `2px` (Forest Green).

---

## 5. Signature Components

### The Ajo Circle
A geometric representation of the contribution cycle.
- **Visual:** A circular array of dots representing members.
- **Progress:** Dots transition from neutral gray to **Forest Green** as contributions are made.
- **Center:** A focal ring highlighting the current cycle or user status.

### OTP Input
High-fidelity, individual digit boxes.
- **Visual:** Separate white cards with 1.5px Forest Green borders on focus.
- **UX:** Auto-focus and native-keyboard handling for a "zero-friction" verification experience.

---

## 6. Tone of Voice

- **Professional:** Secure, accurate, and transparent.
- **Approachable:** Helpful, clear, and community-focused.
- **Avoid Jargon:** Use "Verify Number" instead of "Initialize Terminal," "Savings Circle" instead of "Contribution Node."
